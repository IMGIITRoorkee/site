LecTut - Android

LecTut is the study portal, developed specially for the R-Land junta with an aim of assisting them to achieve their academic goals. So, whether it is a tutorial sheet, lecture slide, exam paper or any other study material, you now have it all in your phone, thanks to LecTut-Android.


The feed page contains the files uploaded in your courses and can be viewed and downloaded to your phone.


The side menu enables you to get the fetch the latest uploaded files, browse courses by department, view your saved files and also displays your Courses.


In a department you can view the courses offered by it and the faculty of the department.


You can view the files downloaded by you in 'My saved files'.

NoticeBoard

Notice Board is an app on Channel i where all the notices related IIT Roorkee are put up. These notices are uploaded by the concerned authorities. Whether it is something related to Placement/ Internship or an academic reform, Notice board is the first place where the information is shared. A lot of paperwork has been reduced since the launch of notice board. This is one of the most vital applications of IIT Roorkee.


You can subscribe to the some specific notices for which you wish to receive email notifications.


You can star notices which makes the task of finding an important notice easy in the future. 


You can select a particular category and find out all the notices related to it. 


There is an Android app available for NoticeBoard which works on the Internet. This makes the notice board easily available to everyone in pockets.
